GST Certificate

Goods and Service Tax Number (GSTIN) is a unified indirect tax which mandatorily requires to register once business annual turnover exceeds INR 40 Lacs. Generally, a small business entity voluntarily applies for GST registration even before crossing the above threshold turnover limit of INR 40 Lacs. GST registration helps a startup to open a current bank account in the name of sole proprietor firm, avail input credit of taxes paid on the purchase, deal with MNCs easily and to sell the product in E-commerce portals like Flipkart, Amazon, and others.
