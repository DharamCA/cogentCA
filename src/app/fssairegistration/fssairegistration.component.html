When Required?

FSSAI registration or food license required if you are into the business of food industry whether manufacturing or processing or packaging or distributing or storage.

Who issues food license?

FSSAI License is issued by the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI), Ministry of Family Health & Welfare, Government of India.

Turnover Based

Food Business with turnover up to ₹12 Lacs shall get Basic FSSAI registration. State license is needed if turnover between ₹12 Lacs and ₹2 Crores. Scroll down to know more.

Location Based

FSSAI Registration or Food License is location based. It requires to obtain multiple FSSAI licenses if food business operates from multiple locations.

State & Central Level Category

FSSAI License categorized into State Level License and Central Food License. Small & Medium Size FBO requires State License and Large Size FBO requires Central License.

Validity & Renewal

FSSAI License is valid for a limited period generally 1 to 5 years. The person requires to apply for FSSAI Renewal before the expiry of the term.

What is FSSAI ?

FSSAI stands for Food Safety and Standards Authority of India which is an independent organisation set up by the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Government of India.

It was formed under the Food Safety and Standards Act, 2006 with sole aim to promote food hygiene and safety in India.

WHY SHOULD I REGISTER FOR FSSAI?

FSSAI registration or food license is required at the very first stage when you enter into the food business. No matter whether you are processing or repacking or just transporting the food, all organisations must require to obtain the food license under FSSAI. The objective behind the FSSAI registration/ food license is to make available safe, pure, wholesome and nutritious food to the consumers.

FSSAI registration is compulsory for all petty food business operators. A petty food business operator is one whose Annual turnover does not exceed ₹12 lakh and Production capacity is less than 100kg per day. The definition of a petty food business operator varies according to his profession and the applicability of food license is decided accordingly. Find out more.

TYPES OF FOOD LICENSE

FSSAI REGISTRATION

This certificate is basic registration to fulfill the requirement of your food startup. Generally, FSSAI Registration fulfill the legal requirement of doing food business in India if your annual turnover is upto INR 12 lakhs

FSSAI STATE LICENSE

Food State License is required if food business annual turnover exceeds INR 12 lakhs or if you’re food caterer

FSSAI CENTRAL LICENSE

Food Central License is required if food business annual turnover exceeds INR 20 crore or if you’re importer or if you are rendering Food catering services in establishments and units under Central Government Agencies like Railways, Air and Airport, Seaport, Defence etc.

How to use FSSAI License number on food product?

If you are selling food product in the market, you must display FSSAI number on your product package in addition to ingredients and flavour used into the product.

What if i failed to obtain FSSAI registration?

In case FSSAI registration/license is not obtained before operating business, the entity will be liable for a penalty upto ₹10 lakhs.

How to verify FSSAI license online?

  • Visit fssai government website
  • Click on FBO Search
  • Enter License / Registration number
  • Verify

What should I do after FSSAI Registration?

After getting an FSSAI Registration, it’s mandatory to display the FSSAI license or certificate at your food premises. Besides, you need to display the FSSAI logo on your product packages.
