KNOW MORE ABOUT TRADEMARK REGISTRATION

What is the difference between trademark, copyright and patent?

  • A trademark is a word, logo, design, and combination of colours & shapes etc. that denotes the originality of a brand and also differentiate it from its competitors.
  • Copyright is an intellectual property that provides rights to a creator over his original literary and artistic works.
  • A patent is exclusive right granted to an inventor over an invention. A patent provides the patent owner the right to commercialise the invention.

What is trademark class?

Trademark classes divided into 45 parts. It describe in broad terms the nature of the goods or services contained in each of the 34 classes of goods and 11 classes of services. So anybody can navigate intuitively through the classes and apply trademark registration based on their business activity.

™ classes is recognised as the international standard classification for Goods and Services. Find out full list of 45 Classes.

How can I do a trademark search?

You can easily search your trademark before spending money on trademark registration

  • Visit government trademark search portal
  • Enter Trademark Class the words, you want to search
  • Press Search

Can I register my same trademark for different kind of business?

There are 45 different classes based on the type of business activity under which you can get trademark registration. You can apply trademark registration under multiple classes at a same time based upon nature of your business activity.
